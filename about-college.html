About Medical College

The college has a modern teaching and material base. Classes are held in the educational building of the medical college in 35 equipped classrooms, including 15 lecture halls, 9 laboratories, 7 computer classes, and 13 equipped classrooms of preclinical practice. In the educational building there is a gym, a reading room for 200 seats, an assembly hall for 500 seats, a modern equipped student canteen. Students live in 2 comfortable dormitories. The integrity of the information support of the educational process is provided by the library. The main professional educational programs are provided with the necessary educational and methodical literature. The educational process also uses textbooks, teaching aids, development, prepared by teachers of the college. The educational process employs 98 teachers, including 15 candidates of medical sciences and doctors of medical sciences. 90% of teachers work with the qualification category, including 73% have the highest qualification category. Honored Doctors and Teachers – 8; Excellent health care – 5, applicants and graduate students – 11. College teachers maintain contact with leading experts of the Ministry of Health of the CBD, participate in regional scientific conferences and seminars on topical issues of health care. For more than 10 years, the medical college actively interacts with the medical faculty of the KBSU – a number of disciplines in the specialties of the medical college are conducted by members of the medical faculty departments of the KBSU. Employees of medical, biological, chemical, and pedagogical faculties of the university, as well as practicing health doctors with high qualification categories and rich practical experience are invited to conduct classes, give lectures.

Scientific-practical conferences of various levels are regularly held in the college. Special attention in the college is paid to SRWS and UIRS, the work of circles, the preparation and conduct of scientific and practical conferences; annual student and faculty conferences; preparation of scientific articles, theses; defense of coursework; scientific cooperation with the medical faculty. For all types of practical training (educational, technological and professional practices), basic treatment-and-prophylactic institutions of the republic, pharmacies, beauty salons are used. Classes are held by cyclic method, which allows students to observe the dynamics of the course of diseases, perform a set of medical procedures, provide care for patients, independently perform manipulations. Working contact with the medical staff becomes close, and what is important, students have the opportunity to study at the bedside. The pedagogical staff of the college seeks to educate not only well-informed, highly qualified specialists, but also moral, responsible, respectable citizens. College teachers face the following tasks: to generate interest in their chosen profession and to prepare students for conscious labor and socially useful activities; to cultivate a sense of moral responsibility for the results of future professional activities; consolidate the skills of independence in labor. Love for the chosen profession, humane attitude to a person does not come by themselves. Lectures, conversations, debates, individual work with students is only part of the educational process. In 2010, students of the MK KBGU for the first time in the history of KBSU became winners of the KBSU festival “Student Spring – 2010”, and in 2013, 2014 and 2015 the students invariably confirmed the status of leaders of the university students, becoming today fourfold winners of the KBSU festival “Student Spring”.
